2013 in Pictures

/ December 31, 2013 /

This year was a full of fun, excitement and achievements. What can be a better thing than a Full Frame camera for a Photographer as  a gift :). And a precious gift “Prisha”, our little princess made this year a special one. A fantastic trekking season covering almost 20 forts was a real fun. Adding to this I won first prize in Trek-Blogs at Girimitra 2013, a trekkers statewide annual get-together and this was great achievement for a Trek-Blogger. And few amazing campings and photo-shoots served as toppings for this year. Here are few moments from this year of a Trek-O-Grapher.

January 2013: A gift and great start of a year for a Trek-O-Grapher

January 2013: First portfolio kinda shoot of year

February 2013: Camping at lake placid

March 2013: A photoshoot at Bhuleshwar Temple

April 2013: Mahaganapti Temple at Wai, my most favorite City

May 2013: Precious gift “Prisha”, Start of fatherhood

June 2013: Awaiting Monsoon and trekking season

June 2013: First trek in season, Bhorgiri

July 2013: First prize award for trek-blog from Trekkers. Thank you all.

July 2013: A monsoon photoshoot

August 2013: A frame that go selected for Drushtikon 2013, a Photography Exhibition

September 2013: Sarasgad, 360 degree picture

October 2013: A street artist in festive season

November 2013: Back into wild. Most memorable sunrise of year 2013

December 2013: The end of year with long awaited Kokan Expedition. 15 forts covered in 4 days.
