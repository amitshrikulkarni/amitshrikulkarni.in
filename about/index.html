Perspective:

Everyone's perception of the world is unique and so it was that trekking, travelling and natural curiosity of the world which ignited my interest in photography. Going back in graduation years, I started clicking casually during travels but this turned into a passion when I purchased a SLR. And since then I am still clicking, clicking and clicking. Photography is an unending journey in a very beautiful world, where one can always see something new, perform something better than before and capture something beautiful. Fortunately God has created this magic world, as a frame with beautiful colours, lights and shadows and full of joy that a photographer can never get bored of his passion to explore and capture.
And my journey has just begun...

Gears:

Reaching out people:

When a photographer captures what he sees into an image, it's obvious that there is urge to share his view with people, to let them see and to let them enjoy, what exactly he did. It is a passion of every photographer to share his world, his perspective with the people. It's a way to grow by learning through sharing. Here is my small effort to share my passion with all of you to let you see "Through My Eyes..."
